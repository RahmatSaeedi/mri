% Chapter 20 - Fig 2: Fringe field, unshielded vs active-shielded (axial view).
% Left: bare magnet, dominant dipole term ~1/r^3, 5 G line reaches ~+-8 m.

%        now falls as the quadrupole term ~1/r^5 and the 5 G line collapses to
