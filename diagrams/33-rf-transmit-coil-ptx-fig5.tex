% Active detuning circuit: a parallel LC trap (detuning inductor L_d in series
% with a PIN diode, placed across the coil capacitor C). With the diode ON the
% trap resonates at f0, presents high Z, and detunes the coil. DC bias reaches
% the diode through an RF choke (passes DC, blocks RF).
